About

Silicon Shelf is a platform for tracking personal reading lists as well as coordinating reading with friends via reading clubs.

A single user can

  • add books to their own log
  • manage their reading list with status updates
  • track dates which they started and completed each book

Users may also create and join reading clubs. This allows them to add a book to all their club members' reading logs.

At this time, Administrative Management of the Database is under development, and user-intended functions are not yet implemented.
